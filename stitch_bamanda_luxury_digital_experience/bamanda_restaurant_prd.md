# TRICODE PRO LTD: Bamanda Restaurant Digital Platform

## Design Intent
A premium restaurant digital experience blending Modern African luxury, Royal heritage aesthetics, Art gallery minimalism, and Natural textures.

## Visual Design System
- **Deep Forest Green (#0F3D2E):** Primary brand color.
- **Royal Gold (#C9A646):** Accent and CTA color.
- **Warm Wood Brown (#6B4F3A):** Secondary texture/backgrounds.
- **Soft Cream (#F8F5F0):** Main background for readability.
- **Charcoal Black (#1A1A1A):** Text and deep contrast.

## Material Inspiration
- Polished wood textures, soft shadows, glassmorphism for overlays.

## Typography
- **Headings:** Elegant serif (Luxury).
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Readability).

## Page Structure
1. **Homepage:** Hero slider, "Now Selling" dishes, Category preview, Brand story.
2. **Menu Page:** Gallery-style dish grid (2-3 columns), sticky categories, search.
3. **Cart Experience:** Slide-in drawer with glassmorphism.
4. **Checkout Flow:** Simple form with WhatsApp redirect logic.
5. **Favorites Page:** Grid of saved meals.

## UX Principles
- Clarity over complexity, fast decision making, low friction (no account required), WhatsApp-optimized checkout.
